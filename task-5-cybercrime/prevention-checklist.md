# Prevention Checklist - Stay Safe Online

## ✅ Actionable Safety Tips

* **Use unique passwords** for all accounts (combine letters, numbers, and symbols).
* **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** on email, social media, and banking apps.
* **Never share OTPs or PINs with anyone**, even if they claim to be from a bank or official authority.
* **Verify links before clicking** – avoid suspicious URLs in emails, messages, or unknown websites.
* **Be cautious on public Wi-Fi** – avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
* **Keep your devices updated** – install regular software and security updates.
* **Download apps only from trusted sources**.
* **Review app permissions** – avoid granting unnecessary access to contacts, camera, or storage.

## 💵 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

* **Set transaction limits** and alerts to monitor unusual activity instantly.
* **Double-check UPI IDs before sending money** – verify the recipient name carefully.

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime

**Contact the following in case of a cybercrime.**
* Report immediately on the **National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in**
* Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930**

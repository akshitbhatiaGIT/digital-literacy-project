# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's ✅

* **Think before posting** – Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
* **Maintain privacy** – Use strong privacy settings and limit personal information sharing.
* **Be respectful** – Engage politely in discussions, even when you disagree.
* **Verify information** – Check facts before sharing news or claims.
* **Build a positive profile** – Share achievements, skills, and constructive content.

## Don'ts ❌

* **Don’t share sensitive information** – Avoid posting phone numbers, addresses, or passwords.
* **Don’t engage in cyberbullying** – Never harass, threaten, or insult others online.
* **Don’t spread misinformation** – Avoid forwarding unverified or fake content.
* **Don’t overshare** – Posting too much personal content can harm your privacy and reputation.
* **Don’t post inappropriate content** – Avoid offensive language, images, or behavior that could impact your future opportunities.
